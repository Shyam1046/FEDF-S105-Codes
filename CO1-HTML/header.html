Welcome to ELDEN RING

Elden Ring is a critically acclaimed open-world action RPG from developer FromSoftware, the creators of the *Dark Souls* series, with worldbuilding crafted by fantasy author George R. R. Martin. Set in the vast and perilous Lands Between, players take on the role of a Tarnished, an exile summoned back to restore order by finding the scattered shards of the shattered Elden Ring and becoming the Elden Lord. The game is celebrated for masterfully blending FromSoftware's signature challenging and intricate combat with an unprecedented sense of freedom and discovery across its massive, seamless world. Players explore breathtaking landscapes, delve into complex dungeons, and battle formidable demigods, all while piecing together a deep and cryptic narrative, cementing its status as a landmark title in the genre.
